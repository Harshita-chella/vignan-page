DEPARTMENTS

CSE

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an engineering discipline that covers several topics related to computation, programming languages, program design, computer hardware and software and integrates several fields of computer science.

ECE

Electronics & Communication Engineering deals with the electronic devices, circuits, communication equipments like transmitter, receiver, integrated circuits (IC). It also deals with basic electronics, analog and digital transmission.

EEE

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) is an engineering course that imparts knowledge related to electrical & machine technology, circuit analysis, telecommunication systems.It deals with the engineering problems like electrical, electronics, computer.


IT

Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, storage, networking and other physical devices, infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, secure and exchange all forms of electronic data.

AI&DS

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the ability of a robot controlled by a computer to do tasks that are done by humans as they require human intelligence and discernment.It is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines.

CIVIL

Civil Engineers are generally responsible for designing, constructing and managing various construction projects such as roads, building, tunnels, bridges.Many Civil Engineers are also involved conducting research.
